About

Filth Chains is a femme-owned chainmail brand specializing in handcrafted accessories and garments that blur the line between the sacred and the profane. Rooted in gothic aesthetics and inspired by religious iconography, each piece merges intricate metalwork with a raw, unapologetic edge.
